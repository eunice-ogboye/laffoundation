About Us

Who Are We?

Holistic empowerment of the African child.

At LAFF, we believe that 'Enlightenment is Empowering.' Knowledge alone is not enough—it must be accessible, actionable, and transformative. Since our founding, we have worked tirelessly to illuminate minds, from street outreaches to school programs, proving that every child holds limitless potential.

Our vission is to see the African child fully recognized and respected as royalty on a global scale.

Our Core Values includes

  • Empowerment – Unlocking potential through education.
  • Equity – Ensuring every child has access to opportunities.
  • Community – Collaborative growth and support.
  • Innovation – Creative solutions for sustainable impact..
